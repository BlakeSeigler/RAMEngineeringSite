FAQs

What positions are you looking for?

We are looking for new members to fill into the Project, Operations (Marketing & Outreach), and the Finance team(s)!

Can I apply to multiple divisions? Do I need to apply separately?

Yes! You can apply to any division(s) you would like to, as the application has options for all branches

How do I apply to be an exec?

Execs are chosen through internal voting available to all club members in the spring. (Execs are appointed for the academic year)

How do Operations & Finance teams work?

All 3 branches are managed by Ram Engineering's Operation and Finance team, which are options on the application form
